Classic Pancakes

Serves 4 · 30 minutes · Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter for frying
  • Lemon juice and sugar to serve

Instructions

Step 1: Make the batter Put the flour and salt into a large mixing bowl. Make a well in the centre and crack the eggs into it. Pour in about 50ml of the milk and the oil. Start whisking from the centre, gradually drawing in the flour.
Step 2: Rest the batter Once all the flour is incorporated, gradually whisk in the rest of the milk until you have a smooth, thin batter. Let it rest for 30 minutes if you have time.
Step 3: Heat the pan Heat a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat and add a small knob of butter. When the butter has melted, swirl it around the pan.
Step 4: Cook the pancake Pour a small ladleful of batter into the pan, tilting and swirling as you do so. Cook for about 1 minute until the bottom is golden. Loosen the edges with a palette knife and flip the pancake over.
Step 5: Finish and serve Cook on the other side for another 30 seconds or until golden. Slide onto a warm plate. Repeat with the remaining batter. Serve with a squeeze of lemon juice and a sprinkling of sugar.
